# Epic 19 — Visual Direction (Art Director)

## Theme Directive

**Underwater world**: Deep blues, teals, aqua, coral accents. Bubbles, fish, seaweed, coral. Playful but not chaotic. Kid-friendly, not scary.

## Color Palette

| Token | Current | New (underwater) |
|-------|---------|------------------|
| --app-bg | Pastel gradient | Deep water gradient (#0d47a1 → #006064 → #00838f) |
| --app-surface | #ffffff | Semi-transparent glass (#ffffff20) or soft teal (#b2dfdb) |
| --app-surface-elevated | #ffffff | Slightly lighter glass |

## Typography

- **Font**: Keep Nunito or switch to something more playful (e.g. Fredoka One for headings, Nunito for body)
- **Weights**: Bold for headings, readable for body
- **Sizes**: Unchanged (base, lg, xl, 2xl)

## Shapes

- Rounded corners (keep --app-radius-*)
- Bubble-like surfaces (soft shadows, subtle gradients)
- Organic shapes for decorative elements

## Icon Style

- SVG line or filled icons
- Underwater-themed: fish, bubbles, seaweed, coral, shell
- Consistent stroke weight (1.5–2px)
- Replace emoji (📚, 📊, ⚙️) with SVG icons

## Background Patterns

- Subtle bubble pattern (repeating circles)
- Optional: gentle wave gradient overlay
- Optional: seaweed silhouette at bottom
- Low opacity so content remains readable

## Do / Don't

- **Do**: High contrast for text on dark backgrounds
- **Do**: Playful but legible
- **Don't**: Pure black (#000) or pure white (#fff) as dominant
- **Don't**: Busy patterns that distract from content
